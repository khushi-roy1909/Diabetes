What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body is unable to properly regulate blood sugar levels due to lack of insulin or insulin resistance.

Common Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Monitor blood sugar
  • Avoid excessive sugar intake
